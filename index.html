About Us

Bridging the Industrial Skills Gap

Located in Lusaka, Rising East Training Institute (RETI) is a TEVETA-accredited trade school committed to equipping individuals with practical, job-ready skills. We focus on enhancing employability and career advancement for artisans, professionals, and youth.

Our Mission

To reduce the skills gap by offering high-quality, hands-on training tailored to Zambia's growing economy.

Our Vision

To empower competent contributors to the job market through accessible and industry-relevant vocational excellence.
